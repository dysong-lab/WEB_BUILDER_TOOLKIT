# Design Context

## Target Audience
Executives and facility managers reviewing data center monitoring dashboards.
Decision-makers who need clarity at a glance, not technical depth.

## Use Cases
- Quick status overview of infrastructure health
- Identifying anomalies and trends
- Presenting system status in meetings

## Brand Personality / Tone
Modern, sophisticated, authoritative.
Confidence through restraint — not flashy, not generic.
The interface should feel like it was designed by a design agency, not generated by AI.

## Design Direction
- **Corporate**: Editorial/magazine influence. Clean hierarchy, generous whitespace, data speaks for itself.
- **Dark Tech**: Industrial/utilitarian. Dense information, monospace precision, operational control room feel.
- **Minimal**: Refined simplicity. Maximum breathing room, near-invisible chrome, content-only.
